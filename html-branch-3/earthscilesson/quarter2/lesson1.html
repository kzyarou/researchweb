- These processes include **weathering**, **erosion**, **transportation**, and **deposition**, all of which play a crucial role in shaping the landscape.

üìù Types of Exogenic Processes:

- **Erosion Agents** include running water, glaciers, wind, and waves, each contributing to the sculpting of Earth's surface in distinct ways.
